Closing or hidding comments

On a per article/page basis you can decide to either show, close or hide the comment section. Just set the pcs meta tag to either open (default), closed or hidden.

  • (Old) Comments are visible
  • Visitors can post new comments

Article/Page:

1
2
Title: My Article or Page Title
pcs: open

Example: Article with open comments

  • (Old) Comments are visible
  • Visitors can not post new comments

Article/Page:

1
2
Title: My Article or Page Title
pcs: closed

Example: Article with closed comments

  • (Old) Comments are not visible
  • Visitors can not post new comments

Article/Page:

1
2
Title: My Article or Page Title
pcs: hidden

Example: Article with hidden comments
